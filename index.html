    I’m a third year Computer Science student at Nantes University. My passions are centered around creative projects, app development, and project management. I typically enjoy and find relative ease in—areas that allow me to use my creativity to find new solutions or improve existing functionalities across various websites and apps. My current project focuses on a worldbuilding app for writers. As someone who enjoys reading both fiction and non-fiction, I’ve noticed that writers need a platform to help organize their notes and thoughts. I enjoy writing myself, but most apps designed for writers often lack key functionalities and organizational features. This is where my project comes in. I aim to create an organized and layered app that allows wiki entries, maps, character designs, language creation, and more, providing a structured and developed environment to support complex projects. Through this project, I hope to develop my abilities and understanding of cloud technologies while practicing database storage and customization. I want to apply both my backend and frontend skills to a project that goes beyond my university curriculum, something I am passionate about and wish to pursue in my free time and as a career. For this project, I plan to use HTML, CSS, Flask, Django, and JavaScript. Once I have a fairly usable and basic version built with Flask, I plan to port it over to a React application.
